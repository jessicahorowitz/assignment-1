The Life and Career of Miley Cyrus

Born Destiny Hope Cyrus, now known as Miley Cyrus, is an American singer-songwriter born in 1992 from Franklin, Tennessee. Cyrus is known for her multifaceted career as a singer, songwriter and actress.

Hannah Montana

Miley Cyrus gained fame at a young age as the star of Disney Channel’s Hannah Montana from 2006-2011. She played the role of a teenage pop star leading a double life as an ordinary high school student. The role catapulted her into the spotlight and led to a successful music and acting career.

Debut Music Career

During her time as Hannah Montana, Miley released her debut album Meet Miley Cyrus in 2007. It was released as a companion to the Hannah Montana 2: Meet Miley Cyrus album, which featured both Hannah Montana and Miley Cyrus tracks. The significance behind the album launched her transition from the Disney persona to a standalone artist

Returning to Her Roots
  • 2017 album Younger Now which marked her return to country and pop music, characterizing herself as reflective and mature.
  • Exploration of new music styles. In 2020 Cyrus’s album Plastic Hearts embraced rock and punk, proving her versatility as an artist and performer.
  • at the 2024 Grammys, Miley won her first ever Grammy for her song “Flowers”
